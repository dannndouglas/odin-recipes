Touchdown Pizza

Home

Description

This touchdown pizza is perfect for game day. Loaded with cheese and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Add pepperoni, bell peppers, and black olives on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
