Sheet Pan Nachos

Home

Description

These sheet pan nachos are loaded with cheese, beans, and all your favorite toppings. Perfect for sharing!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag tortilla chips
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced jalapenos
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Spread tortilla chips evenly on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle black beans and cheddar cheese over the chips.
  4. Add jalapenos and tomatoes on top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with cilantro and sour cream before serving.
