Philly Cheese Steak Dip

Home

Description

This Philly cheese steak dip is creamy, cheesy, and loaded with steak and peppers. Perfect for dipping!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ribeye steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 2 cups shredded provolone cheese
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the steak, bell pepper, and onion over medium heat until the steak is browned and the vegetables are tender.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked steak and vegetables with the provolone cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, and mayonnaise. Mix well.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the dip is hot and bubbly.
